Elden Ring: Nightrein

Nightrein is the most recent addition to the world of Elden Ring, and it takes place in an alternate dimension compared to the original game and its DLC. The NightLord is born from the darkness and destroys the Erdtree before the Shattering happened. The NightLord begins to take over the Lands Between now Limveld and begins to start a new age. This makes Marika call out to the eight Nightfarers which are various soldiers, rangers, priests, and more to combat the Night and bring back the Golden Order. in the end, the Nightfarers are able to succeed in combating the NightLord to stop his age of stars. This age of stars the Nightlord wanted to create is the same as the ending you get by helping Ranni in the base game.

Fun Facts About Elden Ring

Here are some Fun Facts about Elden Ring!

  1. Eating too many dragon hearts from the Dragon Communion can turn you into Magma Wyrm
  2. The Author of Game of Thrones, George R. R. Martin helped create the story behind Elden Ring
  3. Sir Gideon Ofnir, has different attacks depending which bosses you defeat before facing him

People can eat dragon hearts to gain powers of a dragon like their fire breath, but if a person would consume too many they would slowly convert into a magma wyrm. The creator of Elden Ring, Miyazaki, worked with George R. R. Martin to help make the story of Elden Ring. Miyazaki even made a weapon that is based upon the world of Game of Thrones. The Grafted Greatsword is one giant sword that looks like the sword throne. Sir Gideon Ofnir is another tarnished that you meet during Elden Ring, and he has the same goal as you which is to become the next Elden Lord. If you defeat Rykard, Malenia, or Mohg Gideon will have attacks that are based upon the boss's attacks.
